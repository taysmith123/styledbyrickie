Meet the Stylist

I’m Rickieria Means also known as Rickie or Riri! I’m a 20 year old wardrobe stylist hailing from Spartanburg, SC. Growing up my Nanny was always a fashion role model for me. Color coordination, patterns, fabric textures, & jewelry sparked my interest.

My passion for fashion began at an early age from dressing up, drawing, styling, and customizing, which continues til this day. I am currently enrolled at South Carolina State University majoring in Fashion Merchandising. After school I plan to continue my fashion journey by networking, experiencing, and creating and eventually enroll in an interior design graduate program.

The ultimate goal of ‘Styled by Rickie’ is to make clients look and feel GREAT at affordable prices! I wish to build confidence of those who lack while still expressing who they are. Remember, presentation is always key!

XO, Pretty Rickie
